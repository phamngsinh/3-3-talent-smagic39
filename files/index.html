About Us

Solutions Architect

33 Talent is a talent acquisition & management services firm specialising in Big Data, Digital and Communications. We provide recruitment, executive search and managed talent consulting services.

Big Data includes Data Science, Technologists and Analytics.

Digital includes Digital & Social Media, Mobile, eCommerce and Publishing.

Communications includes PR, IR, Corporate Communications and Marketing.

As Technology, Media and MarComms lines merge in the digital world we help customers find the best global talent to keep them ahead of the competition.

From our offices in Sydney and Singapore we provide cutting edge Recruitment and Executive Search solutions to employment brands wanting to go beyond traditional advertising channels and the active candidate market, to strategically target the best talent in Asia Pacific.

Employers can access active talent cheaply and easily, but passive talent (i.e. those that aren't looking for new opportunities) are a lot more difficult to source, engage and manage. Our specialist recruiters work with top talent in the passive talent market. Our business model revolves around developing deep pools of passive Talent with a laser like focus on our specialised verticals and for the following industries:

  • Technology Vendors
  • Digital and eCommerce
  • Media and Publishers
  • Professional Services
  • Agencies
